# ⚖️ VidhaanBot — Indian Legal Assistant

## Live Demo
🚀 Deployed App: https://vidhaanbot.streamlit.app

AI-powered legal research assistant built using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), semantic search, and LLMs to answer questions grounded in Indian legal documents.

---

## Features

- Conversational legal chatbot
- RAG-based legal retrieval
- Source-grounded answers
- Streaming responses
- Conversation memory
- English + Hindi support
- Quick legal topics
- Source citations

---

## Try these sample queries

- What is Article 21?
- Explain Article 14.
- What is deficiency in service?
- What is product liability?
- What protections exist for women at workplace?

---

## Tech Stack

Python • Streamlit • FAISS • LangChain • Groq • Llama 3.3 70B

## Disclaimer

This is an academic legal research tool.  
Responses are generated from indexed source documents and are **not a substitute for professional legal advice.**
